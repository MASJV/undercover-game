How to Play

  • 1Enter 3–12 player names and tap Start Game.
  • 2Pass the phone privately to each player to see their role:
    🟢 Civilian — gets the main secret word.
    🟠 Impostor — gets a similar but different word.
    ⚪ Mr. White — gets no word at all and must bluff blind!
  • 3Discussion: Take turns describing your word in 1–2 words only. Don't say the word itself!
  • 4Vote: Pass the phone round — each player votes for who they think is the Impostor or Mr. White. The most-voted player is eliminated.
  • 5If Mr. White is eliminated → they get one last chance to guess the Civilians' word. Correct guess → Mr. White wins!
  • 6Civilians win only when BOTH the Impostor and Mr. White are eliminated.
  • 7Bad guys win if the Impostor + Mr. White together equal or outnumber the remaining civilians.
  • 💡Tip: Mr. White has no word — they must piece it together by listening carefully to everyone else's clues!
